## FAQ

### 1. Does this require connecting billing accounts?
No. The MVP works entirely through manual input and pricing normalization. No OAuth or billing integrations are required.

### 2. Are the savings recommendations AI-generated?
No. All financial calculations and optimization recommendations are deterministic TypeScript rules. AI is only used to summarize already-validated outputs.

### 3. What kinds of tools are supported?
The audit currently supports major AI tooling platforms including Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, and API-based usage.

### 4. Is company data publicly visible?
No. Public report URLs intentionally strip private metadata, emails, and internal payload details before rendering shared reports.

### 5. Who is this built for?
The product is primarily designed for startup founders, engineering managers, and infrastructure owners trying to manage rapidly growing AI tooling spend.
